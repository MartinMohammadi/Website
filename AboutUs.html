Where We Started

In June of 2017, our team devoted themselves to becoming the number one car wash and detailing service in all of Canada. Starting in a parking garage, we offered high-class cars a cleaning service they would never forget.

Where We Are

What started as a small team has now become the best car cleaning service in Gatineau and Ottawa. We provide a detailing service like no other in a welcoming and ambient warehouse thanks to our high-end equipment such as an industrial pressure washing machine and vacuum, detailing tools such as detailing brushes and microfiber cloths, and our special soap that uses the safest chemicals that guarantee zero harm to your paint.
